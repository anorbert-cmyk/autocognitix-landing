## 3. HOW IT WORKS (3 STEPS)

### Headline
**A Complete Diagnosis in 3 Minutes. Without the Expertise Required.**

### STEP 1: Enter Your Vehicle + Error Code or Describe Symptoms
**Icon Suggestion:** 🚗 or 📝 (input/form icon)

Incomplete information is totally fine—the AI is smarter than you'd expect. Even a vague description gets you in the right direction.

**Icon Suggestion:** 🧠 or ⚙️ (processing icon)

Behind the scenes, your AI does three things simultaneously:

- **Runs RAG Retrieval**: Pulls the most relevant expert information from 35,000+ embedded vectors
- **Integrates Real Parts Prices**: Connects current market prices from Hungary's top suppliers

Result: Not just a diagnosis, but a complete diagnostic blueprint.

### STEP 3: Detailed Report + Cost Estimate Ready to Review
**Icon Suggestion:** 📊 or ✓ (checkmark icon)

- **What's the Problem?** Clear explanation (not technobabble)

Then you decide. Get a second mechanic opinion. Or if you find an honest shop, show them the report—it starts a real conversation.

## 9. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### Q: Do I Really Not Need Hardware?
**A:** Completely true. The AI only needs an error code (like P0303) or a symptom description. No OBD2 scanner, no Bluetooth device, no proprietary hardware whatsoever.

### Q: Can the AI Diagnose Every Car Problem?
**A:** The AI works best with DTC codes. If you give a code + symptoms, accuracy is 95%+. If you only say "weird noise," the AI will list possibilities but will need more data. That's normal and honest.

### Q: How Accurate Are the Parts Prices?
**A:** Daily-updated market data from 15+ Hungarian suppliers. Accuracy is ±10%. (Bulk discounts vary by supplier, so your actual price may differ, but the estimate gives you a realistic ballpark.)

### Q: What If My Mechanic Disagrees with the Diagnosis?
**A:** That's fine. It happens when information is incomplete or the mechanic has run their own diagnostics (like on a dyno). AutoCognitix doesn't replace shop diagnostic tools—it gives you a second opinion, which every car owner should have.

### Q: Does This Work on All Cars?
**A:** 8,145+ models in the knowledge graph—basically every vehicle sold in Hungary since 1995. Most newer vehicles use standardized codes, so coverage is very good.

### Q: How Private Is My Data?
**A:** Your diagnostics are stored on secure, encrypted servers. We never sell your data, never share with third parties. Pro users have explicit privacy terms—completely transparent.

### Q: When Does the Mobile App Launch?
**A:** Q2 2026. Join the early access waitlist now to get first access and special founding pricing.

### Q: Do You Support Electric Vehicles?
**A:** Yes. EVs have different issues (battery, inverter, thermal management), and they're included in our 8,145+ vehicle models.

### Q: Do You Recommend Specific Shops?
**A:** Pro users get access to our network of certified repair shops committed to transparent pricing. Verified reviews from other users. No shop can "pay" for a better rating—only actual customer feedback.

### Q: What If the AI Can't Diagnose My Issue?
**A:** The AI never pretends to know something it doesn't. If it's uncertain, it tells you: "85% confident, but need more data." It will never guess or give a false diagnosis with fake confidence.
