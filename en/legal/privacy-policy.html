1. Introduction

AutoCognitix ("Company", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our AI-powered vehicle diagnostic platform ("Platform"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise handle your personal data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR/EU 2016/679), ePrivacy Directive, and Hungarian data protection laws.

ℹ️ Applicability
This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the AutoCognitix Platform, including EU/EEA residents. If you are located outside the EU/EEA, your data may still be subject to similar protections under your local laws.

6. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject, you have the following rights under GDPR Articles 15-22:

6.1 Right of Access (Article 15)

You have the right to obtain confirmation of whether we process your personal data and to receive a copy of your data. Submit a request to privacy@autocognitix.hu and we will respond within 30 days.

6.2 Right to Rectification (Article 16)

You can correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data. You can update your account information directly in your profile, or contact us for assistance.

6.3 Right to Erasure (Article 17)

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data under certain conditions. We will delete your data unless we have a legal obligation to retain it. Erasure requests may take up to 30 days to process.

⚠️ Important Note on Erasure
Deleting your account will remove your diagnostic history and profile. Some data may be retained for legal, tax, or fraud prevention purposes. Anonymized data may continue to be used for platform improvement.

6.4 Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18)

You can request that we limit processing of your data while we verify its accuracy or assess your other rights. Restricted data will only be processed with your consent or for legal claims.

6.5 Right to Data Portability (Article 20)

You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used format (e.g., CSV, JSON) and to transmit it to another controller. Request this from privacy@autocognitix.hu.

6.6 Right to Object (Article 21)

You can object to processing based on legitimate interest or direct marketing. We will cease processing unless we have a compelling legal reason to continue.

6.7 Right to Withdraw Consent (Article 7)

If we process data based on your consent, you can withdraw it at any time. Withdrawal does not affect the legality of processing before withdrawal.

6.8 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@autocognitix.hu
  • Mail: AutoCognitix Kft., Budapest 1234, Utca 123

We will verify your identity and respond within 30 days (extendable by 60 days for complex requests).

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to countries outside the EU/EEA with different data protection laws. We ensure adequate safeguards using:

8.1 Transfer Mechanisms

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) - EU Commission-approved clauses in Data Processing Agreements with all non-EU processors
  • Adequacy Decisions - Transfer to UK (post-Brexit adequacy decision)
  • Binding Corporate Rules (BCRs) - If applicable for international transfers

8.2 Transfers to Specific Countries

  • United States (Railway, Google, Meta) - SCCs + supplementary technical measures (encryption, anonymization)
  • United Kingdom (Neo4j) - UK Adequacy Decision (post-2023)
  • European Union (Qdrant) - No additional safeguards needed
ℹ️ Your Rights Over International Transfers
You have the right to request information about data transfers and the safeguards we employ. For concerns about international transfers, contact the Hungarian Data Protection Authority (NAIH) or your local DPA.

9. Data Security

9.1 Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption - TLS/SSL encryption for data in transit; AES-256 for data at rest
  • Authentication - JWT-based authentication with secure password hashing (bcrypt)
  • Access Control - Role-based access control (RBAC) to limit data exposure
  • Firewalls - Network-level protections and DDoS mitigation
  • Intrusion Detection - Real-time monitoring for suspicious activity

9.2 Organizational Safeguards

  • Data Protection Officer (DPO) overseeing compliance
  • Employee training on data protection and privacy
  • Background checks for staff with data access
  • Data Processing Agreements with all processors
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing

9.3 Data Breach Response

In case of a data breach, we will:

  1. Assess the scope and severity of the breach
  2. Notify affected users within 72 hours (where required by law)
  3. Report to the Hungarian Data Protection Authority (NAIH)
  4. Take immediate remedial action to prevent further exposure
  5. Provide guidance on protective measures for affected individuals
⚠️ Limitation of Security
While we implement industry-standard security measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute protection against unauthorized access. Users should maintain strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication when available.
