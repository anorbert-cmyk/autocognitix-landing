Which workshop should you visit?

What to look for when choosing a workshop?

  • Ask about their OBD diagnostic tools — a modern workshop should have them as standard
  • Request a written quote for labour and parts before work begins
  • Check Google reviews (4.0+ is the minimum to consider)
  • Ask if they have experience with your specific car brand
  • Find out if they offer a warranty on their work (6 months minimum is expected)

What to tell the mechanic

Sample script for calling

"The diagnostics show fault code [DTC CODE], which indicates [DESCRIPTION]. Can you schedule an inspection?"

Important addition

Always describe the symptoms too (e.g. "the check engine light is on and the car is shaking at idle"). Ask about the estimated cost and turnaround time.
