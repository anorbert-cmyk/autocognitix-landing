Frequently asked
questions

Answers to the most common questions about AI diagnostics, data protection, and usage terms.

Do I need an OBD2 diagnostic tool?
No. AutoCognitix works without any hardware. Just type in the trouble code or describe the symptoms and the AI takes it from there. If you do have an OBD2 reader, you can feed in its data for an even more precise diagnosis.
How accurate is the AI diagnosis?
Our system is built on 26,800+ knowledge-graph nodes and 35,000+ semantic vectors, combined with NHTSA recall data. Diagnostic accuracy is above 97%, but we always recommend a professional workshop confirmation for serious faults.
Does it work in English?
Yes — AutoCognitix works in both English and Hungarian. On the Hungarian side we use the SZTAKI HuBERT model for native-language understanding; the English interface uses an equivalent diagnostic LLM pipeline. Describe symptoms in either language and the answer comes back in kind.
How do you protect my data?
We built a GDPR-compliant system. Your data is stored encrypted, never sold to third parties, and you can request deletion at any time. Diagnostic data is only used in anonymised form to improve the system.
As a workshop, can I generate customer reports?
Yes — on the Business plan. You can generate professional PDF reports for your customers covering the diagnosis, recommended repairs and estimated costs. Customers see it, understand it, and trust you.
Does the app replace a mechanic?
No — and that's not our goal. AutoCognitix is a diagnostic aid that helps you understand the problem. For serious faults we always recommend a qualified mechanic. Simpler repairs (spark plug, fuse, etc.) you may well be able to handle yourself with the DIY guide.
